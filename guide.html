User Guide

Everything you need to know to play and win.

Goal & Basics

1
Objective: Clear the entire board by submitting valid words until no tiles remain.
2
Select letters: Pick any tiles (no adjacency rule). Minimum word length is 3.
3
Submit: Removes the top letter from each selected stack. Used letters are returned to the bag to keep the letter distribution stable.
4
Lose condition (optional): If any stack reaches the chosen threshold (Off / 5 / 7 / 9), the run ends.

Spawning & Warnings

1
New letters spawn on a timer based on difficulty. The next-spawn countdown is shown at the top.
2
Tiles that will receive the next spawn are highlighted with a dashed warning outline shortly before the spawn.
3
Word length affects tempo: shorter words speed future spawns; longer words slow them (details below).

Difficulty & Settings

A
Difficulty (spawn rate × quantity):
  • Easy: 1 letter every 10s
  • Medium: 1 letter every 7s
  • Hard: 2 letters every 5s
  • Insane: 3 letters every 4s (3‑letter words may increase next spawn to 4)
B
Tempo (based on submitted word length):
  • 3 letters: speeds up (shorter interval)
  • 4 letters: neutral (base interval)
  • 5+ letters: slows down (longer interval)
C
Grid Size (4–10): larger grids allow longer sessions; the letter bag scales to maintain variety.
D
Lose at Stack: Off / 5 / 7 / 9. If any stack reaches the threshold, the run ends.
E
Dictionary Validation: Words are checked online via dictionaryapi.dev. An internet connection is required for validation.

Controls

1
Click / tap tiles to toggle selection.
2
Enter: Submit word  •  Escape: Clear selection.
3
☰ Menu: Settings / Reset / Home.
4
From the home page, “Start Game” opens the Settings modal first so you can configure rules.

Tips

✓
Use longer words when the board is crowded to slow spawns and regain control.
✓
Watch pre‑blink tiles to anticipate which stacks will grow next.
✓
Experiment with grid size and threshold to tailor session length and difficulty.
