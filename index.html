About the Game

1
What it is: a fast, mobile‑friendly word puzzle. Make words from any tiles (no adjacency). Clear every tile to win.
2
How it plays: select letters (min 3). Submitting removes the top letter from each selected stack. Tiles that will spawn next show an on‑tile circular countdown. Press Drop (▼) to spawn one letter instantly and reset the timer.
3
Levels & tempo: choose Level 1–20. Higher levels drop more/faster (1→2→3 tiles; L19: 3 @ 4s; L20: 4). Word length adapts tempo: 3 → faster, 4 → base, 5+ → slower.
4
Settings & scores: fixed board 5×6. Optional lose at stack 5–10. Local Scoreboard (☰ Menu) saves your level times and supports sharing.
